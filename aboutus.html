About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Feed Forward, where we are dedicated to alleviating hunger in our community through our food bank management system. Our goal is to ensure that no one goes to bed hungry by distributing food to those in need.

With the help of volunteers, donors, and partners, we have built a robust system that allows us to efficiently manage and distribute food donations while maintaining the highest levels of transparency and accountability.

What We Do

At Feed Forward, we provide an innovative solution to food insecurity. We manage donations from local businesses, farms, and individuals, and distribute them to people in need through our well-organized platform. Our efforts have provided meals to thousands of families and individuals.

We also collaborate with other organizations to reduce food waste and increase food distribution efficiency. Our platform allows us to track donations, manage inventory, and ensure that the food reaches the right people on time.
