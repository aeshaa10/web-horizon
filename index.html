Feed Forward is a dedicated non-governmental organization (NGO) focused on addressing food insecurity and connecting volunteers with those in need. Our mission is to bridge the gap between generous donors and individuals facing food shortages. Through a network of compassionate volunteers, we efficiently collect, package, and distribute food to families and individuals in our community. We believe that everyone deserves access to nutritious meals, and our initiatives aim to ensure that no one goes hungry.

The volunteers of Feed Forward are the backbone of our organization, embodying compassion and commitment as they tirelessly work to collect food from local donors in their neighborhoods. With a strong sense of community, these dedicated individuals traverse their areas, reaching out to generous donors who wish to contribute to our mission of alleviating hunger.Their selfless efforts not only provide immediate relief to those facing food insecurity but also inspire hope and solidarity, making a tangible difference in the lives of countless individuals.

The families supported by Feed Forward represent a diverse cross-section of our community, each with their own unique stories and challenges. Many of these families face food insecurity due to job loss, medical emergencies, or other unforeseen circumstances that make it difficult to put meals on the table. Feed Forward steps in to provide essential nourishment, ensuring that parents can feed their children without the added stress of financial strain. Each family receives carefully curated food packages that include fresh produce, pantry staples, and other nutritious items, allowing them to prepare balanced meals.
